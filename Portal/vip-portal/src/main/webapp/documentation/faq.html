FAQ and troubleshooting

Q: All my jobs are failing. What can I do?

A: open your simulation in simulation monitor and check the job minor status in tab "Jobs Summary"
  • Minor status is "Application execution failed": the simulator didn't manage to process your data. Right-click on the job and check for error messages in the application output and error files.
  • Minor status is "Input download failed": maybe the input file you specified doesn't exist. If it does, then it may be unavailable: contact us!
  • Minor status is "Output upload failed": maybe there is a mistake in the output directory that you specified. If not, contact us!
  • Minor status is "Retrieving status": information about the job is not available yet.

Q: Some jobs failed. Is it an issue?

A: your jobs will be resubmitted a few times (3). Less than 10% of job failures may only indicate some turbulences on the execution system (e.g. a file is not accessible from 1 computing site or the simulator doesn't work properly on 1 site); you should get your results. If the error rate grows beyond 10% then check the previous answer.
