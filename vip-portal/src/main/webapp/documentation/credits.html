VIP project

The Virtual Imaging Platform is a French project initially funded by ANR under grant ANR-09-COSI-013 for a total of 975keuros.
Partners:
  • CEA-Leti
  • Creatis
  • INRIA Bretagne Atlantique (IRISA, VISAGES team)
  • maatG (France)
  • Université de Nice Sophia-Antipolis (I3S, modalis team)

ReproVIP Project

The ReproVIP Project was a French ANR project funded under grant ANR-21-CE45-0024-01 and aiming at evaluating and improving the reproducibility of scientific results obtained with VIP.
