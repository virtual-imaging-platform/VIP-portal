Privacy policy

Name of the Service

Virtual Imaging Platform (VIP).

Description of the Service

VIP provides services for the distributed execution of scientific applications in the field of medical imaging at https://vip.creatis.insa-lyon.fr

Data controller and a contact person

INSA de Lyon, Email : dpo@insa-lyon.fr

Jurisdiction and supervisory authority

France, Commission Nationale de l'Informatique et des Libertes - CNIL. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) provides a framework for the processing of personal data on the territory of the European Union.

Personal data processed and the legal basis

A. Personal data retrieved from EGI Check-in (when logging in with EGI Check-in)

  • Name
  • Family name
  • Email
  • Voperson_verified_email

B. Personal data gathered from VIP

  • Name
  • Family name
  • Email
  • Institution
  • Country
  • User activity on VIP (login, executions, files transfers)

Purpose of the processing of personal data

The email is used for sharing information with the users. Information such as "country" and "institution" can be used for statistics. Logs (user activity on VIP) can be used for security reasons.

Third parties to whom personal data is disclosed

No personal data is disclosed to third parties. Statistics (deprived of any personal information) are shared with partners, stakeholders and the community. Technical staff managing the various services used within VIP may have access to some of the pieces of information described in sections A and B.

How to access, rectify and delete the personal data and object to its processing

Contact VIP support team at vip-support@creatis.insa-lyon.fr

Data retention

1. Personal data. Personal data requested upon registration (account creation in VIP) is handled according to RGPD rules and French laws and regulations (https://www.cnil.fr/professionnel). All personal data is deleted upon user request or if the email address associated with the VIP account is no longer valid (VIP account suppression).
2. Data uploaded by users on the platform. VIP is not intended to be used as a long-term storage platform. Once their project has concluded, VIP users are responsible for the suppression of the data they may have uploaded on VIP. Data uploaded on VIP may be stored and transferred on machines distributed worldwide without warranty of any kind.
